Installation process is simple please follow all the instructions correctly till the end for a smooth activation...

*****Installation instruction: *****

1) Click on Download Button OR Visit www.setup.office.com

2) Sign in with your microsoft account or create one if you don't have it already.

![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/hb2021_img1.png)

3) Enter the Product license key and Select Region-INDIA.
![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/hb2021_img2.png)

4) Download the setup and install the Microsoft Office Home and Business 2021 for macOS.

![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/hb2021_img3.webp)

5) Wait for fews mintues (Usually installation takes 10-20 mins)

6) Open any microsoft office app like Word or Excel, Sign in with the account on which the key was redeemed.
.
6) Your Microsoft Office Product Will be activated

7) If it prompts to activate the office then select I have an product key and then enter the same product license key which you have generated.

![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/hb2021_img4.png)
