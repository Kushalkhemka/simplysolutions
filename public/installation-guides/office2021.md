*****## Installation instruction:*****

1) Click on Download Button to download the Office Professional Plus 2021

2) Download the Office Professional Plus ISO file (about 4.7 GB)

3) After installation, double click to open (ProPlus2021Retail.img) and click on setup file to install the Office 2021

4) Wait until the installation of Office 2021 gets completed.

![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/office2021_img1.jpg)

5) Open any microsoft app after installation such as word, excel, outlook (recommended) , access(recommended)

6) As soon as the app is opened it will prompt to enter license key. Enter above license key and click on activate office.

![](https://supabase-supabase2.exxngc.easypanel.host/storage/v1/object/public/product-assets/installation-guide-images/office2021_img2.png)

7)  If you don't get the option to enter product key, sign out the all the logged in account in application from top right corner

8) Now, Click on file menu > Click on Account Option from bottom left corner. 

9) From Right Side, Click on Activate Option under licensing details >> Enter the Product License Key (mentioned above) 

10) After entering product key , click on "**I want to activate the software by telephone**" (THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT STEP , ACTIVATE ONLY VIA TELEPHONE METHOD by following below steps OTHERWISE YOU MAY FACE ERROR)

**> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTE - ****

Then select: "I want to activate the software by telephone" and click "Next"
![](https://i.postimg.cc/MZfS9FSV/Whats-App-Image-2024-07-09-at-16-40-13-c8daeab0.jpg)

3) In the next window, in the drop-down menu, select your country of location in step 1, for example, we will have the India.
Then, ****scroll up this page**** and you will get the option to enter the installation id (like shown below) which is currently reflecting on your screen.

![](https://i.postimg.cc/pLRybHWg/Screenshot-2024-07-09-194825.png)

4) Enter the installation id, column by column as visible in your screen.

![](https://i.postimg.cc/50MwDrry/Whats-App-Image-2024-07-09-at-16-40-14-ef6470c1.jpg)

5) After entering your installation id on this website page, wait for 3-4 mins while your confirmation id gets generated from the MICROSOFT server. 

6) After, the confirmation id visible on this page, enter it the columns reflected on your screen and then click on Next Button. Voila! Your Office Professional Plus 2021 for lifetime is activated
